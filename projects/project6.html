Transitional program

Human migration is a natural right, yet modern borders often turn it into a contentious issue.

In response to the influx of economic migrants and homeless individuals in New York City, our project offers transitional housing, language and job training to support reintegration.

By embracing these groups, the initiative fosters cultural diversity, local economic growth, and inclusive community development.

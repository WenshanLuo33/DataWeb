Enclave and Studentification Study

The architecture design under theplanning ofthe above urban strategy

This campus exhibition building in Yorkhill, Glasgow, was developed under the “point and line” urban strategy to showcase University of Glasgow student work and foster dialogue between students and the public.

The project aims to highlight student creativity while inviting visitors to enjoy the surrounding landscape.
